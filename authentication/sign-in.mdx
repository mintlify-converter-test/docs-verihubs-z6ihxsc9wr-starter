After [signing up](/docs/sign-up)
 and verifying your email, you can sign in to [Verihubs Dashboard](https://app.verihubs.com/login)
 right away.

Just put your email address and password, and click Sign In. You will be redirected to Dashboard Landing Page. Everything you need will be available on the [dashboard](/docs/dashboard)
. Before you decided which services will be best suit your company needs, you can easily try our Demo). Anytime.

![1881](https://files.readme.io/2ff6f83-SignIn.jpg "SignIn.jpg")

Sign In Page

After you sign in, please check on the right top corner at the header dashboard. Your company name will be displayed below your name as the example below

![](https://files.readme.io/5ee7e8a-3-assigned.png)

If your account is not yet assigned to any company, you will see the information of "Unassigned Company" as in the image below. In this case, you will need to contact your company's administrator to invite you and assigned you to a role to access Verihubs' Dashboard features.

![](https://files.readme.io/b93d8a0-3-unassignedcompany.png)

If this is the first time your company get in touch with us, then you will need to contact our Team through [Email](mailto:j.sukimaja@verihubs.com)
 or [Whatsapp](https://api.whatsapp.com/send/?phone=6285171136799&text&app_absent=0)
 to activate your account and get access to all of our Products. If you are the first user that is assigned to your company, then by default you will have a “Super Admin” role that can access and configure all the roles in the Dashboard (see details on: [Company Access](/docs/company-access)
).

You need to create an [Application ID](/docs/applications)
 for trying Demo. Click Next to know how.
